 XAsmTools - About

What is purpose of this site?

This site describes x server protocol  programming for assembly programmers.  It attempts to promote assembler by showing that some programmers can easily produce programs and enjoy the process.  All programs and tools here are in assembler.  A secondary goal is to show that non-graphical programming is efficient and well suited to some applications.  It may not look professional, but it gets the job done.


Who developed this site?

All programming today borrows heavly from the past.  The tools, ideas, and most code is based on the work of others.  Everything here is presented in this spirit and is free to use.  The primary site author is jeff owens.

 

How do I contact someone for help or suggestions?

LinuxAsmTools has a email discussion list at yahoo called DesktopLinuxAsm.  It can be accessed through email or from the web.  ( http://groups.yahoo.com/group/DesktopLinuxAsm )  This list has little traffic and works as a low spam email account.
